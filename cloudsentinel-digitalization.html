Getting Started

Your Digital Transformation Roadmap

You do not have to do everything at once. Start with what gives you the most immediate benefit.

01

Get Registered

CAC registration, TIN, and SCUML (where required) give your business legal standing and open doors to banking, grants, and contracts.

02

Build an Online Presence

A simple website or Google Business profile makes you findable. Customers research online before they buy — be where they look.

03

Digitise Your Records

Move sales, expenses, and customer data into a spreadsheet or simple accounting tool. Visibility is the first step to control.

04

Accept Digital Payments

Add bank transfer, POS, or QR payment options. Businesses that make it easy to pay collect faster and lose fewer sales.

05

Automate One Process

Pick one repetitive task — invoicing, stock alerts, or appointment booking — and automate it. Then build from there.
