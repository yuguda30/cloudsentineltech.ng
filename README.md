## Who We Are

**CloudSentinel Technologies** is a cybersecurity and digital solutions company based in **Katsina, Northern Nigeria**. We help small and medium enterprises (SMEs), NGOs, schools, and institutions protect their digital assets, achieve regulatory compliance, and embrace digital transformation — at a pace and cost that works for them.

We bridge the gap between sophisticated enterprise-grade technology and the practical realities facing organisations across Nigeria. Our work is hands-on: we do not just advise, we implement.

---

## What We Do

### 🛡️ Cybersecurity Services
Protection against phishing, ransomware, data breaches, and social engineering attacks. Security audits, staff awareness training, and incident response for organisations of all sizes.

### 📋 Compliance & Registration
CAC business registration, TIN, NIN integration, SCUML compliance, and regulatory advisory. We help businesses operate legally and with confidence.

### 🚀 Digital Transformation
End-to-end digitisation — from paper records to cloud-based operations. Web and mobile application development, business management platforms, and marketplace solutions tailored to the Nigerian market.

### 📊 Data Analytics & Insights
Custom dashboards, reporting systems, and data strategy for SMEs and NGOs. We turn your numbers into decisions — without requiring a data science team.

### ☁️ Cloud Solutions
Azure cloud infrastructure, migration, and management. Scalable, secure, and cost-effective cloud setups designed for Nigerian business conditions.

## Our Approach

> *"We do not sell technology. We deliver outcomes."*

CloudSentinel Technologies was founded on the understanding that most technology providers in Nigeria focus on large corporations in Lagos and Abuja — leaving SMEs, NGOs, schools, and Northern Nigerian businesses underserved.

We work directly with founders, directors, and operations teams to understand the real problem before recommending any tool or service. Our solutions are practical, locally contextualised, and built for longevity.
